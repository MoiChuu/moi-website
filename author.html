About Moi

「你的心魔會帶你去到你該去的地方。」

　

你可能難以想像——
一個看到血會暈、小強一飛就想逃命的女子，
居然能寫出讓人發抖的故事。

我寫黑暗奇幻、獵奇恐怖的小說，偶爾會畫圖。
接觸過占星，最近更開始摸索塔羅牌的世界。

看起來樣樣都碰、樣樣不精，但那就是我。
因為不精，故事才會亂中帶真，像人生一樣 ♪

✦

You might find it hard to imagine—
a woman who faints at the sight of blood and flees at the first sign of
a cockroach can write stories that send shivers down your spine.

I write dark fantasy and horror, and I sometimes draw.
I’ve dabbled in astrology, and recently started exploring the world of tarot.

I try a bit of everything, master of none—but that’s just who I am.
Because when things aren’t perfect, stories can feel messy and true, just like life. ♪
